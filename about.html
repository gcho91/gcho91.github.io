About Me



I'm a front-end web developer based in the NYC area. In addition to code, I speak four spoken languages: English, Japanese, Korean, and French.

My journey as a web developer started when I was still attending my undergraduate International Marketing program at Baruch. I had always loved working with small businesses to solve branding problems that included web design, digital marketing, and social media strategy. As I worked on my freelance consulting business, I decided to take the plunge and move to Dallas for three months to attend DevMountain, a 13-week full-stack web development program.

When I'm not coding, you can catch me reading a lot of Murakami books and recording music for YouTube and Soundcloud.
