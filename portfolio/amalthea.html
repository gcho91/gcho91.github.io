About:

Amalthea Coffee is a new coffee roaster e-commerce initiative pioneered by the owner of Tenafly Coffee Company, a New Jersey-based local specialty roastery. The Squarespace-based website was designed and built with two goals: 1) empowering small-business owners to be able to own their entire coffee supply chain from roaster to customer, and 2) making the site a hub for all things related to brewing and enjoying specialty roasts with featured blog and learn section.
